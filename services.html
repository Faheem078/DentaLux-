Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?
+
We recommend visiting every 6 months for routine cleanings and check-ups. Patients with certain conditions may benefit from more frequent visits — we'll advise what's best for you personally.
Is teeth whitening safe?
+
Yes, professional teeth whitening is very safe when performed by dental professionals. We use clinically-tested, enamel-safe whitening agents calibrated to your specific tooth sensitivity.
How long do dental implants last?
+
Dental implants can last a lifetime with proper care. The implant post is permanent, and the crown typically lasts 10–15+ years before needing replacement due to normal wear.
Do you accept dental insurance?
+
We accept most major dental insurance plans and are in-network with many carriers. Our team will help you maximize your benefits and explain your coverage before treatment begins.
What is the Invisalign process like?
+
Invisalign begins with a 3D scan of your teeth. We create a custom series of clear aligners worn for 22 hours/day, changed every 1–2 weeks. Treatment typically takes 6–18 months depending on complexity.
Is cosmetic dentistry painful?
+
Most cosmetic procedures involve minimal discomfort. We use advanced local anesthetics and offer sedation options for anxious patients to ensure a completely comfortable experience.
How much do veneers cost?
+
Porcelain veneers typically range from $1,000–$2,500 per tooth. The exact cost depends on the number of veneers, the complexity, and materials chosen. We offer financing options to make them accessible.
Do you offer sedation dentistry?
+
Yes, we offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas), oral sedation, and IV sedation for patients with dental anxiety or those undergoing complex procedures. Your comfort is always our first priority.
