Our Beginnings

From One Vision
to a Legacy

DentaLux began in 1999 when Dr. Michael Chen returned from Harvard with a bold vision: to create a dental practice that combined clinical excellence with genuine compassion — a place where patients felt cared for, not just treated.

What started as a two-chair practice in West Hollywood has grown into a 8,000 sq ft state-of-the-art dental center with 18 specialists, serving over 12,000 patients and recognized nationally for our outcomes and patient experience.

Our philosophy remains unchanged: every patient deserves a smile they're proud of, and every treatment should reflect the highest standards of modern dentistry.

Our Journey

Milestones in Excellence

1999
The Beginning
Dr. Michael Chen opens DentaLux with two chairs and a team of three, dedicated to providing Beverly Hills with world-class cosmetic dentistry.
2005
Expansion & Specialization
Expanded to a full-service dental center, adding orthodontics, oral surgery, and pediatric dentistry departments with five new specialists.
2012
Technology Leadership
Became the first dental practice in Southern California to implement full digital workflow including 3D CBCT imaging, digital impressions, and same-day crowns.
2018
National Recognition
Named one of America's Top Dental Practices by Dental Economics magazine. Reached the milestone of 10,000 patients served.
2024
The Future of Dentistry
Launched our AI-powered treatment planning system and opened a second location in Santa Monica, continuing our mission of accessible dental excellence.
